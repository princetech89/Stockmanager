1. Dashboard Overview

Inventory Management

Product Information

Each product requires a unique SKU, name, description, category, and pricing information. HSN codes are mandatory for GST compliance.

Stock Tracking

The system tracks available quantity, reserved quantity, and minimum stock levels. You'll receive alerts when stock falls below minimum levels.

Categories & Organization

Organize products using categories and tags for better inventory management and easier searching.

Order Processing

Creating Orders

Create sales or purchase orders from the Orders page. The system automatically calculates totals, taxes, and updates stock levels.

Order Status

Orders can be in Pending, Processing, Shipped, Delivered, or Cancelled status. Update status as orders progress through fulfillment.

Order Management

View order details, print invoices, track payments, and manage customer information from the order management interface.

Billing & GST

GST Compliance

The system automatically calculates CGST/SGST for intrastate and IGST for interstate transactions based on customer GST numbers.

Invoice Generation

Generate professional invoices with all required GST details, company information, and payment terms.

Tax Reports

Generate GST reports for filing returns and maintaining compliance with Indian tax regulations.

Reports

Sales Reports

View detailed sales analytics, revenue trends, and performance metrics across different time periods.

Inventory Reports

Track stock movements, identify slow-moving items, and monitor inventory turnover rates.

Financial Reports

Generate profit/loss statements, tax reports, and financial summaries for business analysis.

Troubleshooting

Common Issues

  • Stock levels not updating: Check if orders are properly completed
  • GST calculation errors: Verify HSN codes and customer GST numbers
  • Reports not loading: Clear browser cache and try again

Performance Tips

  • Regularly update stock levels to maintain accuracy
  • Archive old orders to improve system performance
  • Use categories and tags for better organization

Data Backup

Regularly export your data using the Reports section. Keep backups of important business information.
